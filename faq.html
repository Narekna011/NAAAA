FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Demo work?

The Demo runs for 5 minutes, stores data locally and is removed automatically at the end.

Can I use my own music?

Yes — in the constructor you can set background music for your invitation.

Can I customize colors and fonts?

Yes — each template supports custom colors and fonts.

How do guests RSVP?

Guests see a RSVP section on the invitation page and can confirm or decline with one click.

Is there a free plan?

Yes — the Demo plan is free for 5 minutes. Economy and Premium plans are available for full access.
